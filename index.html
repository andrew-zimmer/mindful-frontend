Welcome to Mindful

Mindful is a mood tracking application that gives a person the means to express their mood, and allow them to explore those feelings to help track patterns that can lead to a deeper selfawareness and the understanding of the root to those emotions.

Not only is this application meant to track your less attractive moods (anger, frustration, confusion), but to also express your better moods (happy, silly, relaxed) as well. It's just as important to understand what makes you feel good and what motivates you, as it is to understand the patterns that lead to those unwanted emotions.
